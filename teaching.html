Teaching

Teaching Philosophy

I believe civil engineering students learn best when theory is connected to real-world practice. Since 2017, I've taught university-level civil engineering by focusing on simplifying complex concepts and grounding every topic in hands-on examples. My goal is to foster curiosity, critical thinking, and a sense of confidence in future engineers through active learning and modern tools.

  • Structural Analysis I & II

Educational Tools & Innovation

  • Created two Android apps:
    • RC Beam: Step-by-step reinforced concrete beam analysis and design walkthrough per ACI 318.
    • Concrete Mix Calculator: Step-by-step mix design procedure using the BRE (DOE) method, with graphical illustrations.
  • Advised student participation in international competitions (e.g. ICAMES)
  • Promoted sustainability and innovation through student clubs at CIU
