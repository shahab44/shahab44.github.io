Asst. Prof. Dr. Shihab Ibrahim

Civil & Structural Engineer | Educator | Researcher | Innovation Advocate

Shihab Ibrahim is an Assistant Professor in Civil Engineering at Cyprus International University (CIU), where he combines over 8 years of academic experience with hands-on project work in structural design and sustainable materials. He holds a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering and specializes in reinforced concrete design, geopolymers, the theory of elasticity and solid mechanics.

Passionate about education, he has delivered numerous certified training courses on ETABS, SAP2000, Revit, and modern surveying tools under the CIU Continuing Education Center. Known for his student-focused teaching style, Shihab links theory to practice through innovative tools and real-world projects — including the development of a mini Proctor hammer and educational Android apps for reinforced concrete beam and concrete mix design .

In parallel with research and teaching, Shihab serves as the advisor to CIU’s Civil Engineering and Sustainability Clubs, and coordinates the university’s Civil Engineering Laboratories. His mission is to bridge academic excellence and practical innovation in the service of sustainable, high-performance construction.

He also shares educational videos on his YouTube channel.
